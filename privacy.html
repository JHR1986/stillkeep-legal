Stillkeep Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13 April 2026
Developer: John Henry Roche, Ireland
Contact: stillkeepsupport@gmail.com

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is handled when you use the Stillkeep mobile application.

Stillkeep is designed to store user content privately using Apple services. Stillkeep does not sell personal data and does not use third-party advertising or tracking services.

2. Data Controller

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the data controller is John Henry Roche, Ireland.

Privacy-related queries may be sent to stillkeepsupport@gmail.com.

3. Eligibility

Stillkeep is intended for users aged 16 and over. If personal data relating to a child under 16 is identified, it will be deleted where appropriate.

4. Personal Data Collected

Stillkeep may process the following categories of personal data:

  • Information provided through Sign in with Apple, such as account-related identifiers and, where available, name or email relay information
  • User-created content, including chapters, moments, text, and photos
  • Technical and account-related metadata required to provide the app, such as record identifiers and timestamps
  • Subscription status information provided through Apple for purchase validation and access to premium features, where applicable

5. Purposes of Processing

Personal data is processed only for the following purposes:

  • Providing and operating the app
  • Authenticating user access
  • Storing, syncing, and displaying user content
  • Enabling subscriptions and premium access, where applicable
  • Maintaining app functionality, security, and reliability
  • Responding to support or privacy-related queries

6. Legal Bases for Processing

Personal data is processed where necessary for the performance of a contract with the user, namely to provide the Stillkeep app and its features.

Certain limited processing may also be carried out where necessary for legitimate interests, including maintaining the security, integrity, and reliability of the app, except where such interests are overridden by the rights and freedoms of the user.

7. Apple Services and Processing

Stillkeep uses Apple services including Sign in with Apple, CloudKit, and StoreKit.

User data is stored and processed using Apple infrastructure in connection with providing the app’s features. Payment card details are not received or stored by the developer.

8. International Transfers

Apple may process personal data outside the European Economic Area. Where relevant, such processing is subject to Apple’s applicable transfer safeguards and legal compliance mechanisms.

9. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the app and maintain the user account, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

If an account is deleted, associated personal data and user content are intended to be permanently removed, subject to any limited technical delay or legal retention obligation that may apply.

10. Account Deletion

Account deletion is available within the app. Deleting an account is intended to permanently remove associated content and account data and cannot be undone.

11. Permissions

If Stillkeep requests access to photos, that permission is used only to allow the user to add images within the app. If permission is not granted, photo-related features may be unavailable.

12. Subscriptions

Stillkeep may offer optional paid subscriptions. Purchases, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled by Apple under Apple’s applicable App Store terms and policies.

13. Data Subject Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may have rights including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, object to the processing of, and receive a copy of their personal data.

Requests may be sent to stillkeepsupport@gmail.com.

14. Security

Reasonable measures are relied upon to protect personal data, including the use of Apple infrastructure and related security features. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

15. Third-Party Services

Stillkeep uses Apple services only, including:

  • Sign in with Apple
  • CloudKit
  • StoreKit

Stillkeep does not use third-party analytics, third-party advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking technologies.

16. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.

17. Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact stillkeepsupport@gmail.com.
